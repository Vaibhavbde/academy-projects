Our Story

Cooking is an act
of devotion.

Ember & Ash was born from a singular belief: that the most extraordinary dining experiences arise at the intersection of rigorous technique and unbridled passion. Nestled in a converted Victorian warehouse, our dining room breathes history while our kitchen pulses with the energy of the avant-garde.

Every dish tells the story of its origin — from the chalk-stream trout to the dry-aged Highland beef. We work directly with fewer than thirty producers, and know every farm by name.
