Our Story

We're a studio,
not an agency.

Born in 2018, VRTX Studio was founded by three designers who were tired of watching great ideas get watered down by committee. We work in small, focused teams — because the best work happens when people care deeply and move quickly.

We've shipped products for startups, Fortune 500s, and everything in between. What stays constant: relentless craft, radical transparency, and zero bullshit.
